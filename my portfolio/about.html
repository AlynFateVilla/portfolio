About Me

I am an artist, designer, and entrepreneur with a passion for creativity. I also run a small business making handcrafted fuzzy wire flowers!

My work explores the fusion of art and technology, pushing the boundaries of traditional design. I am constantly evolving my skills to create visually compelling and interactive experiences.

Outside of work, I find joy in exploring new artistic techniques, reading, and connecting with like-minded creatives.
